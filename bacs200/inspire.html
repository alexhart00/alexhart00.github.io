Toussaint Louverture

When it comes to fighting for what is right, I know we are all in agreement when it comes to the idea that slavery is an inheritally evil act, and those who fight against bondage will be seen as a hero to most,but heroes to all who have been in bondage,that is why Toussiant Louverture is my hero.

Loverture is quite the remarkable person, in history his accomplishments are

  • He fought as a general during the hatitan revolution, to liberate his people
  • He was borned into bondage as a son of an eductated slave, and would later become literate
  • He was great at navigating the intricate power struggles between the many political factions of his time.
  • He was arrested and imprisoned in 1802, but never lost hope for his people, or his fight

Haiti legacy of being a former enslaved colony that inhabitants rose up, and was able to win independence and institute freedom for all people living in Haiti is a legacy that is still very strong today, as many people look toward haiti as the place where the enslaved rose up against their oppressors and won, serving as a example to all oppressed people.
